Precision. Innovation. Compassion.

Yousafzai Medicare Lab & Research Center stands as a modern diagnostic and research facility committed to scientific excellence, patient trust, and community well-being. With advanced laboratory technology and a team of skilled professionals, we provide accurate, reliable, and affordable diagnostic services to communities across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. Our center is dedicated not only to clinical testing but also to pioneering research that supports better healthcare outcomes.

  • Clinical Microbiology & Infectious Disease Diagnostics
  • Cutaneous Leishmaniasis Diagnosis & Research
  • Molecular Testing (PCR-based diagnostics)
  • Advanced Semenology & Male Infertility Investigations
  • Special Chemistry, Hematology & Parasitology
  • Research & Development in novel therapeutic approaches
